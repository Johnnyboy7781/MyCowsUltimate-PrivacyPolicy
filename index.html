PRIVACY POLICY FOR MYCOWS ULTIMATE

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application MyCows Ultimate (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Sforzando Games. The Application is a roadtrip game meant to be played one person in the car who is also NOT driving. MyCows Ultimate asks players to spot and claim a number of different objects that are generally common on roadtrips. Claiming these objects will increase the level of the player's cow. The player with the highest level cow at the end of the game is the winner.
